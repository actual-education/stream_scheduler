How the homework queue works:

1. Upload a screenshot of your problem and your username
2. Your question will appear in the live queue
3. Students can view the queue and upvote questions
4. Questions are selected live on stream using a semi-random spinner wheel.

The spinner wheel gives every question a chance to be selected, but the odds increase over time:

• The longer a question has been waiting in the queue, the higher chance of it getting picked
• Questions with more upvotes also increase the chance of being picked
• Upvotes can be added once every 3 minutes per question
